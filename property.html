Property Overview

A refined ranch on a beautifully maintained acre.

1 Woodland Drive combines a comfortable single-level lifestyle, bonus finished lower-level space, extensive renovations, and an unusually capable detached shop.

The Home

Built in 1974, the home has the strength and presence of a traditional ranch while offering the fresh finishes buyers expect today. The remodeled kitchen, main-floor laundry, finished lower level, and updated exterior make this a true move-in ready property.

The lower level adds flexible living space, including an office with an adjoining private bath.
