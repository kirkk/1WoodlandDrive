Property Overview

A classic ranch with uncommon flexibility.

Set on a full acre in Augusta, this renovated ranch combines established construction, modern updates, outdoor living, and one of the most versatile detached shop buildings in the market.

Thoughtfully updated throughout.

The home offers approximately 2,700 square feet with additional finished lower-level space. The main level includes updated living areas, a fully remodeled kitchen, new appliances, and a new washer and dryer conveniently located on the main floor.

  • Built in 1974 with traditional ranch construction
  • Freshly painted inside and out
  • Fully remodeled kitchen with solid-surface counters
  • Main-floor laundry with new washer and dryer
  • Finished lower level with office and adjoining private bath
