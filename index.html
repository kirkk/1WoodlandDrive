Welcome Home

Classic craftsmanship, renewed for modern living.

Originally built in 1974, this sturdy traditional ranch has been extensively refreshed inside and out while preserving the scale, setting, and character that make it special. The home sits on a beautifully maintained one-acre lot with mature trees, updated landscaping, landscape lighting, and irrigation supplied by a private well.


Inside, the property offers updated living spaces, a fully remodeled kitchen, new appliances, convenient main-floor laundry, and finished lower-level space including an office with an adjoining private bath.

The Shop

More than a garage.

The detached shop is one of the defining features of this property. Designed to visually complement the residence, it provides generous vehicle capacity, a workshop setting, climate control, finished walls, insulation, a bathroom, and a separate rear bay for mower or lawn equipment storage.
